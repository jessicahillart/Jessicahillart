Which two websites are you most inspired by?

Dieter Rams: 10 Principles for Good Design

The Message is Medium Rare

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size or balance?

Dieter Rams: 10 Principles for Good Design

Dieter Ramsâ€™ famous principles can be applied to all disciplines of design and this site presents them in an almost print-like layout with a reduced color scheme and grid.

The Message is Medium Rare

A blog of sorts about two designers eating a hamburger every week and trying to extract a design metaphor from them. It is easy to read with a simple, intentional layout to present the lesson.

What makes them similar and different?

Similar

The size, spacing, and bold typography create an extremely clear visual hierarchy. They are both examples of clean design where simplicity can speak volumes.

Different

Dieter Rams: 10 Principles for Good Design doesn't have a traditional navigation bar at the top, but rather a navigation button on the upper right side. This presents the website more like a magazine.

The Message is Medium Rare is a more traditional site, although the navigation bar is beneath the text header, which does break from conventional norms. However, it does have a lot of information below the fold, which can be difficult to read.
